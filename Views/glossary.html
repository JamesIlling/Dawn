Portable X-ray Fluorescence (pXRF)
This is a non destructive scientific test, which can used to determine the element composition of a sample. This is achieved by bombarding a sample with X-Rays, which causes electrons to jump to higher energy shells. These electrons quickly drop back down to the lower energy electron shells and emit the excess energy as a photon of light a particular wavelength depending on the element.
